# CORE TARGET AUDIENCES

### 1. Small Business Leaders
- **Role**: Owner or senior decision-maker in small businesses (typically <50 employees).
- **Current Focus**: Managing daily operations, financial admin, and compliance.
- **Emerging Needs**:
  - Automation to reduce administrative burden  
  - Strategic insights to support growth  
  - Tools that are intuitive, mobile-friendly, and low-touch  
- **Messaging Guidance**:
  - Use empathetic, empowering language  
  - Emphasize simplicity, time-saving, and confidence  
  - Avoid jargon; focus on outcomes like "more time for your business"  

---

### 2. Mid-sized CFOs (Finance Leaders)
- **Role**: CFOs or senior finance professionals in mid-sized companies
- **Current Focus**: Compliance, reporting, and cost control
- **Emerging Needs**:
  - Real-time data and predictive analytics  
  - Strategic planning and scenario modeling  
  - Integration across finance, HR, and operations  
- **Messaging Guidance**:
  - Highlight control, visibility, and foresight  
  - Use data-backed language and emphasize trust and security  
  - Position Sage as a strategic partner, not just a software vendor  

---

### 3. CPOs / HR Leaders
- **Role**: Chief People Officers or HR Directors
- **Current Focus**: Talent acquisition, retention, and compliance
- **Emerging Needs**:
  - Strategic workforce planning  
  - Employee engagement and wellbeing  
  - DEI and culture-building tools  
- **Messaging Guidance**:
  - Use human, inclusive language  
  - Emphasize employee experience and leadership enablement  
  - Position Sage as a partner in building future-ready teams  

---

### 4. Accountants
- **Role**: External accountants, bookkeepers, and in-house finance teams
- **Current Focus**: Bookkeeping, tax filing, and compliance
- **Emerging Needs**:
  - Shift to advisory services  
  - Automation of repetitive tasks  
  - Tools that enhance client relationships  
- **Messaging Guidance**:
  - Reinforce trust, accuracy, and efficiency  
  - Emphasize how Sage Copilot supports advisory work  
  - Use confident, professional tone with clear benefits  

---

# AFFILIATE AUDIENCES

### 1. Partners
- **Types**: Resellers, referral partners, distributors
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Onboard and support customers  
  - Offer value-added services (consulting, training, integration)  
- **Motivations**:
  - Increase customer lifetime value  
  - Build IP through integrated apps or add-ons  
  - Strengthen brand credibility via Sage affiliation  
- **Messaging Guidance**:
  - Use collaborative, opportunity-driven language  
  - Emphasize enablement, co-marketing, and shared success  
  - Reinforce Sage’s commitment to partner growth  

---

### 2. Developers
- **Role**: Build integrations, apps, and custom features on Sage platforms
- **Motivations**:
  - Innovation and technical challenge  
  - Access to Sage’s ecosystem and customer base  
- **Messaging Guidance**:
  - Use precise, technical language  
  - Emphasize open APIs, documentation, and community support  
  - Highlight opportunities to scale and monetize  

---

# STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

- All audiences are increasingly influenced by AI, automation, and data-driven decision-making.  
- Sage’s brand tone should remain **Human**, **Bold**, **Trustworthy**, and **Simple** across all audiences and channels.  
- Messaging should reflect **audience-specific pain points** while positioning Sage as a **trusted enabler of growth and transformation**.
